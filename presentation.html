The Challenge

Why We Built This

Our production workflows were scattered across spreadsheets, ClickUp, and manual processes — making it hard to track, measure, and improve.

📋
Fragmented Data
Case information spread across ClickUp, Google Sheets, and manual logs with no single source of truth.
⏱️
Manual Effort
Weekly & monthly reporting required hours of manual data compilation by every manager.
📉
No Visibility
Performance metrics, YouTube analytics, and team output had no real-time visibility or consistency.
NB Dashboard 03 / 12
Origin Story

How the Idea Evolved

Identified the Gap

Managers were spending significant time on repetitive data collection — pulling numbers from ClickUp, building spreadsheets, and manually computing ratings every week.

Built the ClickUp Sync Engine

We created an automated sync that pulls all case data — statuses, assignees, custom fields, subtasks — directly from ClickUp into a structured database in real-time.

Added Performance Scoring

A dynamic formula engine was developed with configurable templates — allowing each role (Writer, Editor, Researcher, QA) to have tailored scoring pillars and weights.

Expanded to Full Platform

Reporting, YouTube analytics, violations tracking, team hierarchy, manager ratings, and leaderboards were added — creating a complete operational hub.

NB Dashboard 05 / 12
Scoring Engine

Dynamic Performance Scoring

Each role has a tailored formula with weighted pillars — fully configurable by admins

P1
Quality
Script / Video quality scores based on QA reviews
P2
Delivery
Turnaround time and deadline compliance
P3
Output
Monthly targets vs actual cases completed
P4
Views
YouTube performance relative to channel baselines
P5
Ownership
Manager rating on soft skills and initiative

Weights, brackets, guardrails, and scoring logic are all configurable per role via Formula Templates. Templates are versioned — enabling A/B testing of scoring approaches without data loss.
